For an **AMS (Application Management System / Awards Management System)** to support **Creative Contests** involving video submissions, photography, design evaluations, and public voting, it would need the following services and capabilities:

### 1. Submission Management

* Multi-format file uploads (images, videos, PDFs, design files)
* Large file upload support (HD/4K video submissions)
* Automatic file validation and format checking
* Submission version control
* Team and individual entry support
* Submission deadlines and late submission controls

### 2. HD Video Streaming Service

* Built-in video hosting and streaming
* Adaptive bitrate streaming (1080p/4K support)
* Video transcoding and compression
* Secure streaming with access controls
* Content Delivery Network (CDN) integration for global performance
* Video preview generation and thumbnail creation

### 3. Visual Gallery & Portfolio Management

* Grid and gallery-based entry displays
* Side-by-side comparison views for judges
* High-resolution image viewing with zoom capabilities
* Portfolio-style presentation pages
* Categorization and filtering of entries
* Multimedia showcase pages

### 4. Judging & Evaluation System

* Custom scorecards and rubrics
* Weighted scoring criteria
* Side-by-side judging interface
* Blind judging capabilities
* Multi-round judging workflows
* Judge comments and feedback collection
* Consensus scoring and ranking
* Tie-breaking mechanisms

### 5. Public Voting Module

* Audience voting portal
* One-vote-per-user enforcement
* IP monitoring and fraud detection
* CAPTCHA and bot protection
* Email or SMS vote verification
* Vote auditing and tracking
* Real-time vote counts and leaderboards
* Geographic voting restrictions (if required)

### 6. Security & Integrity Controls

* Anti-bot protection
* Fraud detection algorithms
* Duplicate account detection
* Secure authentication and authorization
* Audit logs for all votes and judging actions
* Content moderation tools
* Copyright declaration and ownership verification

### 7. Workflow Automation

* Contest launch and scheduling
* Automated notifications and reminders
* Submission confirmation emails
* Judge assignment automation
* Voting period management
* Winner announcement workflows

### 8. Collaboration & Review Features

* Internal review committees
* Judge discussion panels
* Shared evaluation notes
* Conflict-of-interest management
* Escalation workflows for disputed entries

### 9. Reporting & Analytics

* Submission analytics
* Voting analytics
* Judge performance reports
* Engagement metrics
* Contest participation statistics
* Exportable reports and dashboards

### 10. Awards & Recognition Management

* Winner selection workflows
* Certificate generation
* Digital badges
* Prize management
* Public winner showcase pages
* Automated winner notifications
