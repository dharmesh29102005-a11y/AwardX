For a **Grants & Funding Management** event/workflow, an AMS (Application Management System) should provide a combination of application intake, review, compliance, audit, and funding management services.

### 1. Grant Application Management

* Grant creation and configuration
* Custom application forms
* Eligibility screening
* Applicant registration and profile management
* Document upload and verification
* Draft saving and resubmission support
* Application status tracking

### 2. Conditional Logic Engine

This is critical for your requirement.

The AMS should support:

* Dynamic form fields based on applicant responses
* Budget limit validation
* Funding amount restrictions
* Eligibility-based question branching
* Automatic rejection for non-compliant applications
* Organization-type specific workflows
* Geographic or sector-based funding restrictions

**Example:**
If requested funding > ₹10,00,000:

* Require additional financial documents
* Route application to senior reviewers
* Trigger risk assessment workflow

---

### 3. Multi-Stage Review Workflow

* Initial screening
* Technical review
* Financial review
* Compliance review
* Final approval committee
* Funding recommendation process

Features:

* Reviewer assignment
* Review queues
* Review deadlines
* Review scorecards
* Approval routing

---

### 4. Conflict of Interest (COI) Management

One of the most important grant-management features.

AMS should provide:

#### Reviewer Conflict Detection

* Organization matching
* Previous collaboration detection
* Manual conflict declaration
* Relationship tracking

#### Reviewer Restrictions

* Block conflicted reviewers
* Automatic reassignment
* Hidden applicant information where required
* Segregated review panels

#### Compliance Controls

* Mandatory conflict disclosures
* Reviewer attestation forms
* Conflict audit reports

---

### 5. Financial Scoring & Evaluation

* Weighted scoring models
* Budget assessment forms
* Cost-benefit analysis
* Impact scoring
* Risk scoring
* Funding recommendation scores

Capabilities:

* Custom scoring rubrics
* Reviewer comments
* Consensus scoring
* Score normalization
* Ranking of applicants

---

### 6. Audit Log System

Essential for government, NGO, university, and corporate grants.

Track:

* Every application submission
* Every field modification
* Reviewer assignments
* Score changes
* Approval decisions
* Budget adjustments
* Funding disbursements
* User login history

Audit logs should contain:

* User
* Timestamp
* Previous value
* New value
* Reason for change
* IP/device information (optional)

---

### 7. Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Roles:

* Applicant
* Reviewer
* Financial Reviewer
* Compliance Officer
* Program Manager
* Funding Committee Member
* Administrator

Permissions:

* View-only
* Review-only
* Approve
* Edit funding allocations
* Export reports

---

### 8. Budget & Funding Management

* Budget proposal submission
* Budget validation
* Funding cap enforcement
* Cost category restrictions
* Multi-year budget support
* Currency management
* Fund allocation tracking

---

### 9. Compliance & Governance

* KYC verification
* Organization verification
* Tax document collection
* Regulatory compliance checks
* ESG requirements
* Legal agreement management

---

### 10. Notifications & Communications

* Application received
* Missing documents
* Review assigned
* Review completed
* Approval notifications
* Funding released
* Grant reporting reminders

Channels:

* Email
* SMS
* In-app notifications

---

### 11. Reporting & Analytics

Dashboards for:

* Applications received
* Approval rates
* Reviewer performance
* Funding distribution
* Geographic impact
* Budget utilization
* Compliance metrics

Reports:

* Grant performance reports
* Audit reports
* Funding summaries
* Reviewer activity reports

---

### 12. Post-Award Grant Management

After approval:

* Milestone tracking
* Fund disbursement schedules
* Progress reporting
* Outcome measurement
* Evidence/document collection
* Renewal workflows
* Grant closure process
