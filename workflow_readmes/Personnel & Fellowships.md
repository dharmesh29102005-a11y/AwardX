For an **Academic Management System (AMS)** to support **Personnel & Fellowships** programs with the capabilities you described, it should provide the following services:

### 1. Nomination & Endorsement Management

* Nomination submission portal
* Third-party nomination workflows
* Nominee invitation and notification system
* Automated reminders for incomplete nominations
* Endorser/referee management
* Nominee acceptance and consent tracking
* Multi-stage nomination approvals
* Collaborative application completion (nominator + nominee)

### 2. Workflow Automation Engine

* Configurable workflow builder
* Conditional routing based on fellowship type
* Automated status transitions
* Task assignment and escalation
* Deadline enforcement
* Approval chains and committee reviews
* Workflow audit trails

### 3. Identity & Access Management

* Corporate Single Sign-On (SSO)

  * SAML 2.0
  * OAuth 2.0
  * OpenID Connect (OIDC)
* Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
* Role-based access control (RBAC)
* Institutional directory integration
* User provisioning and deprovisioning
* Secure session management

### 4. Fellowship Application Management

* Dynamic application forms
* CV/resume uploads
* Supporting document management
* Eligibility validation
* Fellowship-specific application templates
* Application version control
* Draft saving and recovery

### 5. AI-Powered Executive Review Tools

* AI-generated applicant summaries
* Research achievement summarization
* Career-impact assessment summaries
* Leadership and service highlights extraction
* Comparative candidate overviews
* Reviewer briefing reports
* Committee-ready executive dashboards

### 6. Review & Selection Management

* Blind or named review options
* Reviewer assignment engine
* Scorecards and evaluation rubrics
* Committee discussion workspace
* Consensus scoring
* Conflict-of-interest detection
* Ranking and shortlist generation

### 7. Communication Services

* Automated email notifications
* In-app notifications
* Reminder campaigns
* Nominee completion alerts
* Reviewer deadline reminders
* Selection and rejection communications
* Bulk messaging tools

### 8. Analytics & Reporting

* Fellowship pipeline dashboards
* Diversity and demographic reporting
* Nomination conversion metrics
* Reviewer workload analytics
* Selection outcome reporting
* Executive-level reports
* Export to PDF, Excel, and CSV

### 9. Security & Compliance

* Data encryption at rest and in transit
* GDPR/Regional privacy compliance
* Access logging
* Audit trails
* Consent management
* Document retention policies
* Secure document storage

### 10. Committee & Executive Portal

* Executive review dashboard
* AI-generated briefing books
* Candidate comparison views
* Shortlist management
* Decision recording
* Meeting preparation reports
* Final approval workflows
