For an **AMS (Application Management System)** to effectively support **Abstracts & Journals** workflows, it should provide the following services and capabilities:

### 1. Submission Management

* Abstract, manuscript, and supplementary file submission portals
* Support for multiple file formats (DOCX, PDF, LaTeX, ZIP)
* Draft saving and resubmission functionality
* Version control for revised submissions
* Automatic submission acknowledgment emails

### 2. Formatting & Compliance Validation

* Strict word count enforcement for abstracts
* Character count enforcement for titles, keywords, and author bios
* Template compliance checking
* Reference and citation format validation
* Figure, table, and attachment validation
* Mandatory field verification before submission

### 3. Plagiarism Detection Service

* Integrated plagiarism screening engine
* Similarity score generation
* Detailed similarity reports for editors
* Threshold-based automatic flagging
* Detection of self-plagiarism and duplicate submissions
* Editorial review workflow for flagged submissions

### 4. Double-Blind Review Management

* Automatic removal of author-identifying information
* Metadata anonymization
* Anonymous manuscript generation
* Hidden author affiliations during review
* Reviewer identity protection
* Blind communication channels between reviewers and authors

### 5. Peer Review Workflow Engine

* Reviewer assignment and matching
* Multi-round review support
* Review deadlines and reminders
* Review form customization
* Scoring rubrics and evaluation criteria
* Editorial decision workflows (Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, Reject)

### 6. Reviewer Management

* Reviewer database and expertise tagging
* Conflict-of-interest detection
* Reviewer invitation management
* Reviewer workload balancing
* Review completion tracking
* Reviewer performance analytics

### 7. Editorial Management

* Editor dashboards
* Submission triaging
* Decision tracking
* Revision request management
* Associate editor assignment
* Editorial board collaboration tools

### 8. Communication & Notifications

* Automated email workflows
* Review invitation notifications
* Deadline reminders
* Revision requests
* Decision letters
* Publication notifications

### 9. Security & Compliance

* Role-based access control
* Secure document storage
* Audit logging
* GDPR and regional privacy compliance
* Data retention policies
* Encryption of manuscripts and reviewer data

### 10. Analytics & Reporting

* Submission volume analytics
* Acceptance/rejection statistics
* Reviewer turnaround times
* Plagiarism statistics
* Journal performance metrics
* Editorial workload reports

### 11. Publication Preparation

* Final manuscript collection
* Copyright and licensing forms
* DOI assignment integration
* Metadata export (CrossRef, Scopus, PubMed)
* Production workflow handoff
* Publication scheduling

### 12. Advanced AMS Features

* AI-assisted reviewer recommendations
* AI-powered submission quality checks
* Duplicate submission detection
* ORCID integration
* CrossRef integration
* Institutional affiliation validation


The AMS must provide:

1. **Submission & Document Management**
2. **Word/Character Limit Validation**
3. **Automated Formatting Compliance Checks**
4. **Plagiarism Detection Service**
5. **Submission Anonymization Service**
6. **Double-Blind Peer Review Workflow**
7. **Reviewer Assignment & Management**
8. **Conflict-of-Interest Controls**
9. **Editorial Decision Management**
10. **Audit Trail & Compliance Logging**
11. **Automated Notifications & Reminders**
12. **Secure Document Storage & Access Control**

These services collectively form a complete Abstract and Journal Management workflow suitable for conferences, academic journals, research organizations, and publishing houses.
