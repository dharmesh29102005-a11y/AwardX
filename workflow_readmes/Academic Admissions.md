For an **Academic Admissions** workflow to function as described, the AMS (Application Management System) should provide the following services:

### 1. Applicant Portal

* Student registration and authentication
* Application form creation and submission
* Document upload and verification
* Application status tracking
* Fee payment integration (if applicable)

### 2. Blind Reference Letter Management

* Automated referee invitation emails
* Secure one-time reference submission links
* Referee identity verification
* Confidential reference storage
* Student access restrictions (references remain hidden)
* Reference reminder and follow-up automation

### 3. Structured Academic Records Service

* Dynamic grade-entry tables
* GPA/CGPA calculation support
* Transcript data import (CSV, Excel, SIS integration)
* Subject-wise grade validation
* Academic qualification templates for different education systems

### 4. Workflow & Review Management

* Reviewer assignment
* Department-specific review workflows
* Multi-stage evaluation processes
* Committee review support
* Decision recording and approval routing

### 5. Data Privacy & Compliance Service

* GDPR compliance support (Europe)
* FERPA compliance support (United States)
* Regional data residency controls
* Consent management
* Data retention and deletion policies
* Privacy audit trails

### 6. Document Management System

* Secure document storage
* Transcript management
* Recommendation letter storage
* Version control
* Digital signature support
* File encryption at rest and in transit

### 7. Access Control & Security

* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
* Applicant, referee, reviewer, and administrator roles
* Multi-factor authentication
* Encryption of sensitive applicant data
* IP and login monitoring

### 8. Evaluation & Scoring Engine

* Admission rubric configuration
* Reviewer scoring forms
* Weighted evaluation criteria
* Ranking and shortlisting tools
* Consensus scoring for committees

### 9. Communication & Notification Service

* Application submission confirmations
* Reference request notifications
* Missing document reminders
* Admission decision notifications
* Bulk email communication

### 10. Reporting & Analytics

* Applicant demographics dashboards
* Application funnel analytics
* Program-wise admission statistics
* Reviewer performance reports
* Compliance and audit reports

### 11. Integration Services

* Student Information Systems (SIS)
* Identity verification providers
* Email providers
* Learning Management Systems
* National education databases
* Payment gateways

### 12. Audit & Compliance Logging

* Complete activity logs
* Reference submission tracking
* Reviewer decision history
* Data access logs
* Compliance reporting exports
